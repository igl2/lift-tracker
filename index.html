Welcome to LiftTracker!

Hello there! You must be on this site for a reason. You're either a beginner looking for a good way to start your fitness journey or a regular gym goer who wants a way to keep track of your workouts!

If you are a beginner, we can help you get started. Let's start off by telling us your preferred workout plan.

What type of workout plan would you like?

Bodybuilding

You will be prescribed a bodybuilding based workout plan. This plan could work on building your muscles through scheduled workouts and meal plans.

Strength

You will be prescribed a powerlifting based work out plan. This plan works on your strength gains and will be require you to be increasing in weights weekly.

Functional

You will be prescribed a crossfit based workout plan. You will train hard with these workouts. This will include workouts that require endurance, strength, and speed.
